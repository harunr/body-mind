In Zhineng Qigong, our minds play an important role in leading a life of balance and well-being. You can start trying this out right here and now:

  • Pay attention to calm, healthy and conducive thoughts
  • Make sure your actions are sincere and full of integrity
  • Balance your emotions
  • Ensure harmonious relationships with yourself and others
