Description

Choose your own time to unwind and heal! This is THE retreat to go inward and rejuvenate from deeply inside and allow your self-healing abilities to progress and prosper! Master Jianshe and his team look forward to welcome you & share their vast wisdom, knowledge and experiences about one of the most powerful healing and well-being science in the world. Are you ready to take your health and well-being in your own hands? Far away from your daily routine, this beautiful Qigong centre will help you to set

„A quiet place of healing and well being, where youthfulness and longevity can be attained through the practice of Qigong. “

This is the meaning of „Hexianju“ - the place to be if you want to create a retreat for health and well-being.

This is also the express purpose of your stay in the Zhineng Qigong Centre of Master Jianshe on the tropical island of Hainan. The daily training with very experienced Qigong masters helps you to find balance and become aware of your centre, to build strength and inner peace, and in doing so, very easily achieving a new form of vitality and well-being

The tropical island of Hainan, which is known as the “Hawaii of the East”. The Qigong Centre of Master Jianshe in the north-east of the island is situated in the Coconut Forest Bay along a lake in the middle of many palm trees. From there it takes only about 15 minutes to the beach on foot.

You are staying in comfortable rooms and practice Zhineng Qigong in a light-flooded training room, on the roof terrace, in the palm gardens or at the lake. You are spoilt with delicious, healthy food and can enjoy the beautiful nature, the blue sky and the clean air. In such an environment you are creating an inward journey in a natural manner, nourish yourselves with Qi and powerfully shape your healing and development process.

Except in the months May, June and July when the centre is close, you are welcome to stay in the centre either as long as you like. You can create a very flexible stay according to your needs e.g. for hree weeks, one or two months and even up to 12 months.

Master Jianshe looks back on more than 25 years of intensive research and teaching of Qigong. As one of the few Zhineng Qigong Masters who were trained by Dr. Pang Ming, he focuses his training on topics of healing, balance, inner peace and personal strength as well as mental power. He worked in the Huaxia Centre, which has become known in the West as the world's first "drug- free" hospital, for many years. He supported the healing of hundreds of Zhineng Qigong students – all over the world.

What you can expect:
  • Learning and deepening of Zhineng Qigong healing methods Level 1 and 2
  • Methods for activating the self-healing abilities
  • Building a powerful and effective healing Qi-field
  • Daily exercises under the guidance of Master Jianshe & other Zhineng Qigong Teaches from China
  • Morning and evening practice on the roof top overlooking the lake and palm trees
  • Methods and exercises can be incorporated into your own daily activities after your retreat
